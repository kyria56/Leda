About Leda Global Sports

Our Mission

Leda Global Sports USA & UK is focused on making competitive Taekwondo more accessible to young athletes. We provide an electronic Protector and Scoring System (PSS) that gives competitors a critical advantage without the high price tag of professional-grade equipment.

What is a Taekwondo PSS?

The PSS is a game-changing technology in Taekwondo. Instead of human judges manually scoring matches, this system uses electronic sensors embedded in protective gear like chest protectors and headgear. When a competitor lands a strike with correct force and placement, the system instantly registers the point.

This technology has become the standard in major competitions like the Olympics because it ensures objective, accurate, and fair scoring, eliminating controversy from human error.

Leveling the Playing Field

Professional PSS systems can be very expensive, creating barriers for aspiring athletes. By offering a more economical version, Leda Global Sports helps young athletes get comfortable with electronic scoring systems and gain a competitive edge in training and tournaments.
